# AISight vs Perplexity: Which AI Answer Engine Respects Your Privacy?

*Both AISight and Perplexity answer questions using AI — but the way they handle your data couldn't be more different.*

## The Key Difference: Where Your Data Goes

**Perplexity** sends every query you type to cloud servers. Your questions are processed by remote AI models, stored on their servers, and potentially used to improve their service. You need an account to use it.

**AISight** processes everything on your device using Apple Intelligence. Your queries never leave your phone or Mac. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no cloud AI services involved.

## Privacy Comparison

| | AISight | Perplexity |
|---|---|---|
| AI processing | On your device | Cloud servers |
| Queries tracked | No | Yes |
| Account required | None | Required |
| Analytics/tracking | None | Yes |
| Data stored on servers | None | Yes |
| Open source | Yes (MIT license) | No |
| Data jurisdiction | EU (Finland) | US |

## Pricing Comparison

| | AISight | Perplexity |
|---|---|---|
| Free tier | 20 searches/day | Limited |
| Pro price | **$4.99 one-time** | **$20/month ($240/year)** |
| Pro features | Unlimited searches, custom server | GPT-4, unlimited, file upload |

AISight Pro is a one-time purchase. No subscriptions, no recurring charges. Perplexity Pro costs $20 per month — that's $240 per year, every year.

## Search Technology

**Perplexity** uses proprietary search infrastructure. You don't know which sources it queries or how results are ranked.

**AISight** uses SearXNG, an open-source metasearch engine that aggregates results from Google, Bing, Brave, and more. The search server has zero logging — no IP logs, no request logs, no analytics.

## AI Quality: An Honest Comparison

Perplexity has access to GPT-4 and Claude, which are more capable for complex reasoning, creative writing, and nuanced analysis.

AISight uses Apple Intelligence (~3B parameters), which runs entirely on your device. It handles everyday questions, research, and fact-checking very well. For complex multi-step reasoning, cloud models may produce better answers.

**The trade-off is clear:** Perplexity offers more powerful AI, but every query you make is tracked and stored. AISight offers privacy by design — your searches are yours alone.

## Who Should Use AISight?

- Privacy-conscious users who don't want their searches tracked
- Apple ecosystem users (iPhone 15 Pro+, iPad M1+, Mac with Apple Silicon)
- People tired of subscription fatigue ($4.99 once vs $20/month)
- Users who want to verify exactly what code runs on their device (open source)
- Anyone in the EU who wants GDPR-compliant search infrastructure

## Who Should Use Perplexity?

- Users who need the most powerful AI models available (GPT-4, Claude)
- Researchers doing complex, multi-step analysis
- People comfortable with cloud-based AI processing
- Users who need file upload and analysis features

## Summary

If privacy matters to you, AISight is the clear choice. Everything stays on your device, the code is open source, and the infrastructure is EU-based with zero logging. If you need the most powerful AI regardless of privacy, Perplexity offers that — at the cost of your search data.
